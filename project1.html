Item Description
Goals Gain proficency in unfamiliar areas of RHCSA exam.
Materials
  • Textbook: Red Hat RHCSA/RHCE 7 Cert Guide by Sander van Vugt

Activities Activities will consist of reading through each section of the material and trying to implement the things explained with the VM.
